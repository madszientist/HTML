My most recent getaway was to an Ashram in the Bahamas. It was a very interesting experience, as it was less a vacation and more of a learning experience. We spent most of our day practicing yoga or helping in the communal kitchen preparing breakfast or dinner for the group of visitors and those enrolled in the YTT program. It was the first getaway to an island country that I did not spend any time relaxing on the beach or by a pool, but it was a wonderful and intraspective experience.
